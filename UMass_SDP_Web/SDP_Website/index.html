Problem/Solution

Remote controlled cars are a popular form of entertainment. However, with the growing fast-paced life our society demands, it can be difficult to get people together.

With our product, this remote controlled car entertainment is possible over the internet. This allows for people to join up remotely and enjoy an immersive experience through soccer.

GAME PLAY

  • Play with up to 4 cars

  • Users can control their car remotely with a first person view

  • Less than 2 instances of physical intervention per game

    • RC CAR

  • Ability to customize at least 20% of the car’s surface area

  • Less than 60ms system delay in the arena

  • Car battery life lasts at least 20 min

  • Car gets stuck less than 2 times per game

    • GOAL POST

  • Only the ball triggers the sensor (99% trigger rate)

  • Automatically returns the ball into the arena (95% success rate)
